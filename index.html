Tools For UNDERSTANDING Highway Performance (TFUHP)

The material presented in TFUHP is developed from various sources, including full scale testing, transportation textbooks, research classified as traffic flow theory, and probability models. The following is an extremely small sample of the resources used in this development:

  • Full Scale Testing: Sugiyama et al. (2008)

  • Textbooks: Banks (1998), Daganzo (1997), Elefteriadou (2014), Trieber and Kesting (2013)

  • Research: Ossenbruggen (2017)

  • Probability Modeling: Iacus (2008), Iacus (2016)
